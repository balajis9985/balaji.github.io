Investment Opportunities in Vellore




In Vellore, there are leather industries, automobile industries, manufacturing
industries and IT Companies.All these contribute to the economy of Vellore.
There are around 12 large and 300 medium scale industries in Vellore district.
There are many small scale industries manufacturing spirit and alcohol, paper bags, flour, drugs,
leather bags and shoes etc.There are sugar mills in Ambur, Thirupathur and Vellore.


Tamil Nadu Industrial Explosive Ltd.
manufactures emulsion explosives etc. and exports to Sri Lanka, Maldives, Egypt, Indonesia etc.
Other manufacturing units like E.I.D Parry Ltd. Ranipet,
Malladi drugs and Pharmaceuticals Ltd. in SIPCOT Industrial Complex Ranipet, BHEL (Bharat Heavy Electrical Ltd.)
have generated employment opportunities for people here.


In recent times, growth has been witnessed in industries manufacturing sugar and jaggery,textiles,
auto parts, fertilizers, food processing, steel castings, synthetic fibres, leather goods etc. in Vellore.
The main occupation of people residing in rural areas is weaving, beedi and matchstick rolling.
Only a few have taken to agriculture.

District Industries Centre




In order to provide services and support to entrepreneurs and address their problems,
District Industries Centre is functional at the district level for implementing schemes launched by Central &
State Government.Registration of all the small Scale Industries is completed at District Industries Centre.

Information Technology in Vellore




IT Companies, BPO Companies, graphic designers, website development and hosting companies are much in demand in Vellore nowadays.
Businessmen are conversant with advantages of making their presence felt globally through the Internet and even make profits from e-commerce.
Man power is available for IT industries as there are a number of technological colleges and training institutes in Vellore.


Today, even patients first visit websites and take online consultation and fixed dates for meeting from their concerned physician before landing in Vellore for treatment.
This saves much time and effort, thanks to information technology.

Why invest in Vellore?!



Vellore is an educational hub and is more famous also for medical tourism.
It is fast growing not only in terms of industrialization and information technology but also the service sector
too is booming and contributing to the economy of this place. With health sector, there is growth in related
businesses such as testing labs, X-Ray clinics, hotels, creches, restaurants etc.
Leather industry is at its best right now exporting a chunk of finished products to foreign lands.There has
been considerable infrastructural development.Other medium and small scale industries too are gaining momentum.
Road and rail connectivity is good. So,the future looks bright for Vellore as far as economy and business is concerned.
