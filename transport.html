Road Transport


  • Buses:


    • Vellore is well-connected by a network of public and private buses. Tamil Nadu State Transport Corporation (TNSTC) and private bus operators run both intra-city and inter-city bus services.


  • Auto Rickshaws:


    • Auto rickshaws are a popular mode of local transportation for short distances within the city. They are readily available and are a convenient way to navigate through Vellore's narrow streets.


    Rail Transport




    Vellore Cantonment Railway Station: This railway station is the primary railhead serving Vellore. It is well-connected to major cities in India, including Chennai, Bangalore, and Coimbatore. Several passenger and express trains pass through this station.

    Private Transport




    Cars and Motorbikes: Private vehicles are a common mode of transportation for residents. Vellore has a network of well-maintained roads, including national highways, making it accessible by car or motorbike.
